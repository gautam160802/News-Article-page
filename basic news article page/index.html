The Rise of AI in Everyday Life

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is no longer a futuristic concept; it has become a part of our everyday lives. From personal assistants like Siri and Alexa to advanced tools for diagnosing diseases, AI is transforming how we live and work.

Tech companies worldwide are racing to innovate in this space, making AI smarter, faster, and more accessible. However, with these advancements come ethical concerns about privacy and data security.

"AI is the new electricity. Just as electricity revolutionized industries 100 years ago, AI is doing the same today." - Andrew Ng
